B.Sc. in Computer Engineering

A comprehensive four-year undergraduate program designed to prepare you for a successful career in computer engineering and technology

Program Overview

  • Duration: 4 years (8 semesters)
  • Total Credits: 140 credit hours
  • Class Size: Maximum 40 students
  • Medium of Instruction: English

Admission Requirements

Academic Background

HSC/A-Level or equivalent with minimum GPA 4.00

Required Subjects

Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry/Computer Science

English Proficiency

IELTS 6.0 or equivalent

Admission Test

Written test and interview

Curriculum Highlights

First Year Core Courses

  • Introduction to Computing 3 credits
  • Programming Fundamentals 4 credits
  • Digital Logic Design 3 credits

Advanced Specializations

  • Artificial Intelligence 4 credits
  • Computer Networks 3 credits
  • Software Engineering 4 credits
