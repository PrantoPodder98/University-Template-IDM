Fall 2024 Semester Registration Deadline

October 5, 2024
Posted by Admin
1,245 views
Important

Announcement Details

All students must complete their course registration for Fall 2024 semester by October 15, 2024. Late registration will incur additional fees. Please ensure you have completed all prerequisites and have met with your academic advisor before registration.

Important Dates

  • Registration Opens: October 1, 2024
  • Regular Registration Deadline: October 15, 2024
  • Late Registration Period: October 16-20, 2024 (additional fees apply)
  • Classes Begin: October 25, 2024

Additional Information

For more information about course registration, please visit the Registrar's Office website or contact your academic advisor. Remember to clear any holds on your account before attempting to register.
